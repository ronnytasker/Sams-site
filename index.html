Sam Tasker is a singer, songwriter and guitarist. Hailing from the wilds of Norfolk, he plays a distinctive blend of high-energy folk rock, punctuated with fiery, incisive lyricism. Away from time spent on his music, Sam enjoys fishing, swimming and writing about himself in the 3rd person.
